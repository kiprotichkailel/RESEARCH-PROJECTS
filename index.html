We employ a variety of methodologies to ensure the accuracy and relevance of our findings. These include qualitative research, quantitative analysis, field experiments, and computational models.

  2. Analysis: Analyzing data using statistical tools and software.
  3. Reporting: Presenting findings through reports, publications, and presentations.
